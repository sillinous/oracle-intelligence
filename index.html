Executive Summary
The AI fitness coaching market represents a high-conviction opportunity at the convergence of three macro forces: consumer health prioritization post-pandemic, the consumerization of AI, and the $100B+ fitness industry's structural failure to serve the majority of gym members who churn within 3 months.
Our analysis identifies a $4.2B SAM reachable within 36 months, concentrated in the 28–45 demographic with demonstrated willingness-to-pay of $29–79/month for personalized digital wellness.

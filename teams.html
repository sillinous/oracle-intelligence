Common Questions
Can I white-label the reports for clients?
Yes — on the Agency Plan, reports can be delivered with custom branding (your firm name, logo, email domain). Contact us to set this up during onboarding.
Do report credits expire?
Starter and Growth packs have no expiry — use them whenever you need them. Agency Plan is a monthly subscription (unused reports don't roll over).
How do team members access shared credits?
On the Growth Pack, we set up a shared team email that receives all reports. Agency Plan includes individual team member accounts with a shared credit pool.
Can I get Strategic-tier reports in bulk?
Yes — Strategic reports ($199 each, or discounted in custom bulk packages) include adjacent market mapping, M&A landscape analysis, and scenario modeling. Contact us for bulk Strategic pricing.
What's the turnaround time?
Same as individual reports: approximately 5 minutes per report. For large bulk orders (10+ reports at once), plan for 45–60 minutes total.
